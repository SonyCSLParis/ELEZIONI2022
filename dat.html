Dati e ringraziamenti

    Per realizzare i grafici abbiamo usato:
  • Scattertext. Per più informazioni vedi “Jason S. Kessler. Scattertext: a Browser-Based Tool for Visualizing how Corpora Differ. Proceedings of ACL-2017 System Demonstrations, 2017, Vancouver, Canada, Association for Computational Linguistics”
  • Plotly Technologies Inc. Collaborative data science. Montréal, QC, 2015. https://plot.ly.
  • D³ Data-Driven Documents. M. Bostock, V. Ogievetsky and J. Heer, "D³ Data-Driven Documents," in IEEE Transactions on Visualization and Computer Graphics, vol. 17, no. 12, pp. 2301-2309, Dec. 2011, doi: 10.1109/TVCG.2011.185.
